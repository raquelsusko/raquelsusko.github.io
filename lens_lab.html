Overview

The goal of this project was to integrate a lightweight robotic arm onto an aerial platform to perform autonomous "pick-and-place" operations. This requires a high degree of stability and real-time vision processing.

Project Details

  • Integrated the SO101 robotic arm with a custom mounting solution for drone stability.
  • Applied SmolVLA for visual-language-action mapping, allowing the drone to identify and approach target objects autonomously.
  • Collaborated on PID tuning to compensate for the shifting center of mass during arm movement.
