REU Student Researcher

During the summer of 2025, I served as an REU Student Researcher in the Imerse Lab at Johns Hopkins University. My work focused on pioneering surgical robotics using Universal Robotic arms and high-precision Zivid cameras.

Project Highlights

  • Developed a teleoperation system in ROS2 for controlling a UR5 arm using a force-dimension haptic control device.
  • Facilitated critical data collection for imitation learning algorithms.
  • Integrated high-fidelity 3D vision systems to enhance robotic perception in surgical environments.

Technical Contributions
